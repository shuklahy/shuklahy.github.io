About Me

I'm a Master's Student in Computer Science at Arizona State University with a predilection towards Data Science, Algorithms and System Software. I completed my undergraduate from College of Engineering, Pune with Information Technology as my major. During this time my primary area of focus was Data Structures and Algorithms, Operating Systems and Computer Networks.

Prior to my master's I worked for 2 years as System Software Engineer at Nvidia wherein I developed, maintained and optimized the code as owner for the crucial component-I2C which is vital for the working of the display driver. I was also summer Intern at Credit Suisse wherein I developed a Java-based application to automate manual based testing in Prime View module. My core competencies are C, Java, Python and Operating Systems
