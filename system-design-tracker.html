How to Practice

  • Framework: Requirements → Capacity Estimation → API Design → Data Model → High-Level Design → Deep Dive → Tradeoffs
  • Time yourself: 35-40 minutes per design from start to finish
  • Draw it: Always sketch the architecture — boxes and arrows matter
  • Know your numbers: QPS, storage, bandwidth estimates off the top of your head
  • Tradeoffs: For every choice, explain why not the alternative
  • Failure modes: What breaks at 10x traffic? During a regional outage?
